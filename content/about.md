## About This Site

Zug Notes is a field notes blog about Swiss crypto regulation, the Crypto Valley ecosystem, and what holding crypto actually looks like in Zug.

Written by someone based in Zug. Not a lawyer, not a financial advisor. Observation and context, not professional guidance.

## What You'll Find Here

- **Regulation**: FINMA policy, MiCA interaction, CARF reporting, and the regulatory mechanics that shape crypto in Switzerland
- **Ecosystem**: Crypto Valley reality — what's here, what's changed, what the outside world gets wrong
- **Holdings**: Swiss wealth tax treatment, capital gains exemption, crypto-friendly banks, and daily holding experience

## What This Is Not

- Legal advice
- Financial advice
- Investment recommendations
- Trading signals or technical analysis

Swiss crypto regulation changes frequently. Always verify with a qualified Swiss lawyer or financial advisor before making decisions.
